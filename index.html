When there's so much platforms, you have to stand out.

These days, all you see is messaging platforms. All of them have their unique quirks, but we strive to be the best one for every good reason.

Secure and Private Messaging

With Eclipse, messaging becomes easier and more secure. Every message sent gets hashed, salted, and cooks at 350°F. On each users end, that lovely message is prepared and served with a little decryption sprinkled on top to make it human-readable.

Realtime

No more waiting for a message to arrive. In Eclipse, every message is delivered in real-time. No matter how far away your friend is, you'll always get their message.

Endless Customization

Eclipse offers users endless customization, including banners, animated profile pictures, custom profile colors — and more, all for the low price of nothing.

100% Free.

Eclipse's features are 100% free, and will always stay that way. If you want to help us keep Eclipse alive, consider donating to us. All proceeds go to making Eclipe better — and you'll even get a badge on your profile.
